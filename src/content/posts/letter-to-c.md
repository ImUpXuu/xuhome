# 关于我在课上给cui写了封信这件事

Dear Mr./Ms. **cui**,

I really want to thank you for being my math teacher. You always help me in many ways, not only in study but also in my life.

Your interesting classes always made me feel happy. My math grade was not so good before I met you. But when I first took your class, you tried your best to make it useful and fun. Slowly, I fell in love with math and my grade became better.

You also helped me a lot in my daily life when I had problems. When I couldn't find my pen, you lent me a new one. You encouraged me when I felt sad. Especially when I was worried about my grades, you listened to my feelings and tried to make me happy.

Although you were strict sometimes, I think you are my best teacher and friend.❤️

Sincerely,
